Danna's Favorite Things

Welcome to Danna's Favorite Things. This page was created to highlight some of the things in life I enjoy. Among my favorite things are great food, good company, travel and when I'm in good shape a nice half marathon.

My Outlook on life can best be described by the quote Efforts and courage are not enough without purpose and direction.

Items which I find are fundamental to purpose and direction are among the following:

Education The willingness to know more
Family A support system
Faith Knowing that everything you do has meaning
